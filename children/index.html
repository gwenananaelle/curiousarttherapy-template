Children

When engaging children in an art psychotherapy session, their imagination, their games and dreams become the language of our conversation. Mixing playing and art making, in a safe environment, the child opens about how she/he perceives the world and the adult therapist can help him/her make sense of it. This unique method reduces the asymmetry in knowledge and power between child and adult, art as a healthy and dynamic transitional space. Happy to talk on how to accompany your child in his/her questions and transitions
