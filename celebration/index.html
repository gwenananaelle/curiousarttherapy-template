Creative Rituals & Celebrations

Life is marked by important transitions, birth, puberty, coming of age, graduation, engagement, marriage, divorce, loss, death, grief... Each of these moments deserves to be honored in a way that feels authentic and meaningful. As an art therapist and celebrant, I help individuals and families design personalized rituals and ceremonies that reflect your cultural heritage, beliefs, and values. These can be simple or elaborate, secular or spiritual, traditional, or entirely new. What matters most is that they resonate with you and your loved ones. Together, we can weave creative expressions and symbolic actions, such as collaborative art-making, poetry, music, or nature-based gestures, into these ceremonies. These elements offer not only beauty, but also healing, connection, and a deeper sense of belonging. Whether you wish to celebrate a joyful milestone or to honor a difficult passage, I will support you in creating a ceremony that is inclusive, original, and deeply personal. Let us celebrate together and transform life’s transitions into meaningful memories that endure

Art therapy can be a gentle, original and inclusive way of celebrating major life events and transitions. Whether individually or in family, unique ceremonies and rituals can be designed, with or without spiritual references and always according to your beliefs and values. Let’s celebrate together and create meaningful memories for you and your loved ones.
